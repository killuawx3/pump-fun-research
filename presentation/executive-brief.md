# Pump.fun — Executive Brief

**Date:** April 2025 | **Category:** DeFi / Token Launchpad | **Chain:** Solana

---

## Overview

Pump.fun is a permissionless memecoin launchpad on Solana that has generated **$1.02B+ in revenue** since January 2024 with **fewer than 10 employees**. It allows anyone to create and trade tokens via an automated bonding curve, with successful tokens "graduating" to its own DEX (PumpSwap). The platform has launched **17.5M tokens** and raised **$1.32B via its PUMP token ICO** in just 12 minutes.

## How It Works

**Bonding Curve Model:** x*y=k with virtual reserves (30 SOL / 1.073B tokens). Price increases ~14.7x from creation to graduation (~85 SOL / ~$69K market cap). Upon graduation, liquidity auto-migrates to PumpSwap with LP tokens burned.

**Revenue Model:** 1% trading fee (bonding curve) + 0.25% fee (PumpSwap) + creation/migration fees. Vertical integration via PumpSwap (Mar 2025) captures full token lifecycle revenue.

## Bottom Line

Pump.fun is the most capital-efficient protocol in crypto history by revenue-per-employee, having built a $1B+ business with minimal team. Its vertical integration strategy (launchpad + DEX + token) creates a powerful flywheel. However, the 98% scam rate and regulatory exposure represent existential risks. The platform's future hinges on whether it can maintain market share, navigate regulation, and sustain revenue through memecoin market cycles.

**Bull case:** Multi-chain expansion + PumpSwap moat → $2B+ annual revenue run rate
**Bear case:** Regulatory action + memecoin fatigue → rapid revenue decline

# pump.fun — Competitive Landscape

pump.fun has dominated the Solana token launchpad market since inception:
- **Peak market share**: 98%+ (throughout 2024)
- **Lowest point**: ~57.5% (May 2025)
- **Current**: ~73.6% (recovered from lows)

## Competitive Dynamics

### What Triggered the Market Share Decline

1. **LetsBonk and Believe** launched with aggressive incentives
2. **Higher graduation rates** on competitors attracted creators
3. **PumpSwap transition** created temporary uncertainty
4. **Fee structure** was less creator-friendly before May 2025 update

### How pump.fun Responded

1. **PumpSwap** — Built own DEX to capture post-graduation volume
2. **Free token creation** — Removed 0.02 SOL creation fee
3. **Creator fees** — Introduced fee sharing to incentivize creators
4. **Zero migration fee** — Eliminated 6 SOL graduation cost
5. **Cross-chain expansion** — ETH, Base, BSC, Monad subdomains registered
6. **Vyper acquisition** — Cross-chain trading terminal
7. **PUMP token** — $1.32B ICO for community ownership

### Current Moat

- **Brand recognition** — "pump.fun" is synonymous with memecoin launches
- **Network effects** — Most users, most liquidity, most visibility
- **Vertical integration** — Creation → Trading → DEX (PumpSwap) → Token (PUMP)
- **Data moat** — 17.5M tokens of historical on-chain data

## The "98% Scam" Problem

According to Solidus Labs research:
- **98% of pump.fun tokens** show scam-like characteristics
- **Only 0.4% of users** have earned more than $10K profit
- **Top 12 tokens** (0.00009% of total) account for 55% of combined value
- Most prolific deployer created **3,357 tokens** with only **16 graduating**

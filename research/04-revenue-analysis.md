# pump.fun — Revenue Model & Financial Analysis

## Revenue Sources

1. **Trading Fees** — ~1-1.25% on bonding curve trades (primary revenue)
2. **PumpSwap Protocol Fees** — 0.05-0.93% depending on market cap tier
3. **Graduation Fees** — 0.015 SOL per token (previously 6 SOL, now negligible)
4. **Token Creation Fees** — 0 SOL (previously 0.02 SOL, now free)

## Revenue Context

For perspective on pump.fun's revenue machine:
- **More revenue than Raydium ($126.9M)** in 2024
- **Comparable to Jupiter ($401.3M)** in 2024
- **Per employee**: With <10 employees, outpaces Kraken ($1.5B revenue, thousands of employees) per capita
- **First Solana protocol to exceed $1B cumulative revenue**

## PUMP Token Buybacks

Nearly 100% of protocol revenue is directed to PUMP token buybacks:
- **Total buybacks**: ~$323.4M spent
- **Supply removed**: 28.8% of circulating supply
- **Strategy**: Aggressive buyback program to support token price
